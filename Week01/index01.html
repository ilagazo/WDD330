Master Mobile UX Course Notes

Rewire Your Brain

  • Design for Mobile UX first because it leads to simple and beautiful apps.
  • UX Mobile design began since 2007 (Intro of the iPhone) and exploded in 2011.
  • Cheaper and faster to do mobile first
  • Desktop computer slaes declining since 2011
  • MOBILE IS NOT A FAD

How to Improve UX by Understanding Users

  • BE THERE. Be there to help them along their decision
  • BE USEFUL. Satisfy their needs quickly
  • BE QUICK. Reduce their cognative load and make it quicker for them.
  • Eliminate steps that are unecessary. 0 steps if possible
  • Reachability. 1 thumb vs 2 thumbs. What can they easily reach with 1 vs 2 thumbs. Make it accesible for them. Key items especially
  • Speed Matters! 2 second delay = 4% drop in revenue.
  • Networks Matter! Ensure that it can hit 4G, 3G, etc.
  • For speed and network, reduce image usage. Change formats to SVG. Optimize and minify CSS and JS files. GZIP files to eliminate unnecessary data.

Better Mobile UX with Strategic Menu Design

  • Ensure main nav bar is within the best reachability.
  • Most commonly used menu items are at the bottom to ensure reachability
  • Doing this creates perception of speed, improved engagement rates
  • Choose icons that everyone will udnerstand. Hamburger menu vs actuall wording Menu. Menu will work better
  • Mobile is new. We need to do research (A/B testing) to see the best way to design

Designing Intuitive and Mobile Friendly Forms

  • Ditch labels and have labels appear inside the form input
  • Use float labels
  • Ensure that checkboxes and fields are larger for thumbs to click on
  • Enable autocomplete for forms. It reduces errors, confusion, and anger
  • Passwords. Show Passwords but offer the ability to hide
  • Numbers (credit). Ensure adequate spacing and clear understanding
  • Ensure keyboard is the best for number or text input. For numbers, use the numberpad.
  • For Phone numbers on apps. Make sure that users can just tap on a number and it calls us. DON'T DO COPY AND PASTE!

Designing a Better Mobile Advertising User Experience

  • Before we advertise, RESEARCH!
  • Popup ads and in-betweener ads are annoying. Avoid them!
  • Video ads are useful and endup in better conversion rates
  • background window ads are good. Don't block users from what they are doing
  • User's don't visit your website to look at ads. Don't use pop-ups!
